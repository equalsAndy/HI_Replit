## Summary

Implement a comprehensive email invitation system that allows admins and facilitators to create, manage, and send rich-text email templates with dynamic variable substitution. Integrates AWS SES for email delivery and AWS S3 for image storage, with a Tiptap-based rich text editor. Facilitators can send emails through any of the three branded sender identities or download pre-formatted HTML to use in their own email client.

**Estimated Effort:** 16-18 working days
